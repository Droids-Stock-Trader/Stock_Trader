About Stock Trader

Stock Trader is a web application for individuals who want an easy, real-time way to monitor the stock market and their investments.The application has the capability to:
  • provide alerts on specific stocks or companies
  • allow the user to make transactions
  • provide links to relevant news articles to keep users up to date

Services We Offer

The application is geared towards users who want quick, anytime, anywhere access to stock market information and the ability to easily make transactions on the go.
